## Ongoing Research Projects

### Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs) for NLO
Developing neural network architectures that respect the underlying physical symmetries of the Nonlinear Schrödinger Equation (NLSE) to improve generalization and reduce training data requirements.

### Computational Modeling of Soliton Dynamics
Investigating the stability and interaction of optical solitons in multi-mode fibers using high-performance numerical simulations in Julia and C++.
