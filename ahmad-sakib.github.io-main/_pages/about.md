I am **Ahmad Hasan Sakib**, a dedicated Physics Scholar at **Shahjalal University of Science and Technology (SUST)**. My academic journey is driven by a deep fascination with the fundamental laws of nature and the transformative power of computational intelligence.

### Academic Profile
My research is situated at the intersection of **Computational Physics** and **Machine Learning**. I am particularly interested in how advanced algorithms can be leveraged to model complex physical phenomena and analyze high-energy particle interactions.

### Core Research Interests
*   **Nonlinear Optics (NLO):** Investigating the behavior of light in nonlinear media and the development of novel optical devices.
*   **Machine Learning in Physics:** Applying neural networks and deep learning techniques to solve physical problems, specifically in high-energy physics and material science.
*   **Philosophy of Science:** Exploring the logical foundations of physical theories and the epistemological implications of computational modeling.

### Technical Expertise
*   **Programming:** Python (NumPy, SciPy, PyTorch), C/C++, Julia.
*   **Tools:** LaTeX, Git, Mathematica, ROOT (CERN).
*   **Specializations:** Numerical simulations, data analysis, and mathematical modeling.

### Contact & Collaboration
I am always open to discussing research ideas and potential collaborations. Feel free to reach out via email or connect with me on professional platforms.
